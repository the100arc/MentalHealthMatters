Why This Matters

Talking about mental health shouldn't feel isolating or shameful. This space was created to help normalize the conversation—especially in faith-based and culturally diverse communities. Inspired by the work of Canadian Muslim Counselling (CMC) and similar organizations, this page brings together trusted mental health and Islamic resources that support emotional and spiritual wellbeing.

Resources You Can Trust

Canadian Muslim Counselling

Virtual therapy services rooted in both psychology and Islamic principles. Their therapists are trained to support individuals, couples, and families.

Naseeha Mental Health

A confidential support line for Muslim youth, with resources on anxiety, relationships, and identity.

Wellness Together Canada

A national hub for free mental health and substance use support, including self-guided courses and live counselling.
